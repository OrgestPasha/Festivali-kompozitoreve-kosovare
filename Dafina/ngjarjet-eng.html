“ ASPIRING COMPOSERS ”

The Kosovo Composers Festival will start with the promotion of new composers with a concert aiming to create a strong network of young creators and encourage the creation and promotion of new musical works. The performance of works by young composers will be further stimulus for new creations and to raise the level of professionalism as well as to add new works of Kosovar music.
